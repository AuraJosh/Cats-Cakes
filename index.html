Our Story

From Passion to Profession

Cat's Cakes began in 2015 when founder Catherine combined her two passions: baking and artistic design. What started as a hobby making custom cakes for friends quickly grew into a full-fledged bakery.

Today, our team of talented bakers creates edible masterpieces that celebrate life's sweetest moments. Every cake is crafted with precision, using only the finest ingredients and innovative techniques.

"Our mission is to make the world a sweeter place, one cat cake at a time."

Giving Back to Our Feline Friends

At Cat's Cakes, we believe in supporting our furry friends in need. That's why we donate 5% of all profits to local cat shelters and rescue organizations. We also host regular "Bake for a Cause" events where customers can purchase special treats with 100% of proceeds going to cat welfare.
